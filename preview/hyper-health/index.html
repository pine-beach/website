Specialised treatments for deeper recovery

Advanced therapies tailored to the way your body moves, heals, and responds.

Cupping Therapy

A time-tested technique using suction to increase blood flow, release muscle tension, and accelerate the body's natural recovery.

Lymphatic Drainage

Gentle, rhythmic massage that stimulates the lymphatic system to reduce swelling, support immune function, and promote whole-body wellness.

Neurokinetic Therapy (NKT)

A precise assessment system that identifies and corrects dysfunctional movement patterns stored in the brain's motor control centre.

Visceral Manipulation

Hands-on therapy targeting the body's internal organs to restore natural mobility, improve function, and relieve referred pain.
