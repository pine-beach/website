Specialist Treatments

Advanced therapies tailored to address pain, improve mobility, and accelerate recovery.

Cupping Therapy

A time-tested technique using suction to increase blood flow, release muscle tension, and accelerate the body's natural recovery.

Lymphatic Drainage

Gentle, rhythmic massage that stimulates the lymphatic system to reduce swelling, support immune function, and promote whole-body wellness.

Neurokinetic Therapy (NKT)

About Hyper Health

Hyper Health Allied Health Care is a multi-disciplinary clinic with two Sydney locations — Marrickville and Wetherill Park — built on the belief that genuine health outcomes come from treating the whole person, not just the symptoms. Our team of chiropractors, physiotherapists, podiatrists, and massage therapists works together with a fully integrated and holistic approach to your care. We take pride in combining high-quality treatment with empathetic, attentive service — because feeling truly heard is part of getting better.
