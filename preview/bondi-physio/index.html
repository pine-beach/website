How We Can Help You

Expert Physiotherapy, Tailored to You.

From acute injury to long-term performance, we offer evidence-based physiotherapy tailored to your body and your goals.

🏃

Sports Physiotherapy

Elite-level sports injury assessment and treatment. Whether you're training for a club final or just want to get back to the activities you love, we have the experience to get you there — fast and safely. Our approach: accurate diagnosis → targeted manual therapy → progressive rehabilitation → return-to-sport testing.

ACL & Knee Injuries Shoulder & Rotator Cuff Hamstring & Muscle Strains Ankle Sprains Overuse Injuries Return-to-Sport Rehab
🦴

Musculoskeletal Rehabilitation

Targeted rehabilitation for muscle, joint, ligament, and bone conditions. We design personalised rehab programs that fix the problem at its source — not just mask the symptoms. We use evidence-based manual therapy, exercise rehabilitation, and patient education to restore strength, mobility, and function.

Lower Back Pain Neck Pain & Headaches Hip & Knee OA Tendinopathies Post-Surgery Rehab Postural Correction
🪡

Dry Needling

A highly effective technique for releasing tight muscles and reducing pain at trigger points. Using fine filiform needles, our practitioners target myofascial trigger points — those stubborn tight spots that cause pain and restricted movement. All dry needling is performed by fully qualified physiotherapists with specific training.

Muscle Tightness & Knots Chronic Neck & Back Pain Headaches & Migraines Plantar Fasciitis ITB Syndrome Post-Workout Recovery
🤝

General Physiotherapy

Not everything is sports-related — and we love that. From postural issues and headaches to pre- and post-surgical rehab, we treat the full spectrum of physiotherapy conditions. No GP referral required. Medicare Chronic Disease Management (CDM) plans accepted. WorkCover and CTP patients welcome.

Meet Our Principal Physiotherapist
Lisa Tomlinson
Principal Physiotherapist · BPhty (Hons)

Lisa Tomlinson is one of Australia's most accomplished sports physiotherapists — and she's been the backbone of Bondi Physio since founding the practice in 1987.

Her career spans more than 1,000 national league games across the NRL, NBL, and WNBL — including her role as official physiotherapist to the Australian Opals, Australia's national women's basketball team. She's worked pitchside at the elite end of Australian sport, managing the most complex injuries under the highest pressure.

That experience is woven into the fabric of how our practice operates. At Bondi Physio, Lisa brings the same elite-level attention to detail, clinical reasoning, and care to every patient — whether you're a professional athlete or someone who just wants to carry the groceries without wincing.
