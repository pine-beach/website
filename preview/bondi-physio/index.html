Bondi Physiotherapy & Sports Injury Centre

Bondi Physiotherapy & Sports Injury Centre has served the Bondi community from 30 Curlewis St since 1987. We don't categorise patients by treatment type — we treat you as the athlete you are. Whether you're managing a desk injury, returning from surgery, or preparing for competition, our approach is built around your goals.

Clinic Story

Bondi Physiotherapy & Sports Injury Centre opened in 1987 at 30 Curlewis St, Bondi Beach — and has been here ever since. Founded by Lisa Tomlinson-Alonso two years after graduating, the clinic was built on a simple principle: every patient deserves the same precision and commitment you'd bring to an elite athlete. Nearly four decades later, that principle still drives everything we do.

Team Highlight

Our principals, Lisa Tomlinson-Alonso and Albert Alonso, have worked the sidelines of the WNBL, NBL, and NRL across more than 1,000 combined National League games — bringing that same precision and commitment to every patient at Bondi Beach.

Values

We believe every person who walks through our door is an athlete — their goals just look different. We bring honesty, precision, and genuine sporting expertise to every consultation. We don't pad treatment plans or overpromise timelines. We get you better, and we tell you the truth about how long it takes.

Services by Athlete Type

We don't categorise patients by treatment type — we categorise them by athlete type. Because how you move, what you're training for, and what you're trying to get back to shapes every clinical decision we make.

Industrial Athlete

You sit for extended periods, commute, or manage physical demands specific to your job. WFH has introduced a new generation of workplace injuries — postural strain, repetitive stress, and ergonomic imbalances that accumulate over time. We assess, treat, and build sustainable movement habits so your body can handle what work demands of it.

Recreational Athlete

Weekend sport, running, gym, ocean swimming — the recreational athlete pushes hard without the recovery infrastructure of a professional setup. We diagnose accurately, rehabilitate thoroughly, and guide you through a structured Return to Play protocol so you get back to what you love with confidence.

Adolescent Athlete

Young athletes face a unique set of challenges: growing bones, multiple sports, high training loads, and pressure to perform year-round. We understand the physiology of developing bodies and provide careful, age-appropriate management to keep adolescent athletes healthy through their formative years.

Professional Athlete

When your livelihood depends on physical performance, clinical opinions must be exact. We have worked at the highest levels of Australian sport and bring that same rigour to elite and semi-elite athletes requiring complex rehabilitation or independent second opinions.

Meet the Team

Lisa Tomlinson-Alonso — Principal & Founder

Lisa graduated in 1985 and founded the clinic two years later. She served as physiotherapist to the WNBL Sydney Flames (1993–2000), the NBL Sydney Kings (1997–2000), and the Australian Opals — accumulating over 400 National League games on the bench. She has also supported GPS Sydney Grammar and CAS Waverley College schoolboy rugby programmes since the 1980s.

Albert Alonso — Principal, Sports Physiotherapy

Albert graduated from Sydney University in 1985 and spent 21 seasons (1988–2008) as physiotherapist to the NRL Canterbury Bulldogs, alongside a stint with the NBL West Sydney Razorbacks. He holds a Masters in Sports Physiotherapy (2002), is a published researcher, an Honorary Research Associate at Sydney University, and served on the ACU committee from 2017 to 2023.

Ivan Liang — Physiotherapist

Ivan graduated from ACU in 2024 and completed an internship with the Cronulla Sharks NRL programme. He specialises in movement biomechanics and completed the UTA22 Blue Mountains ultramarathon in 2025. Ivan consults in English and Cantonese.
