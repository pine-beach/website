Our Massage Services

From targeted therapy to total relaxation — choose the treatment that's right for your body.

💪

Deep Tissue Massage

Release deep muscle tension and chronic knots.

Targets the deeper layers of muscle and connective tissue. Ideal for persistent aches, postural issues, and recovery from physical activity. Firm pressure, real relief.

🩺

Remedial Massage

Therapeutic treatment for pain and injury recovery.

Assesses and treats muscles, tendons, and ligaments to support the body's natural healing process. Perfect for sports injuries, workplace strain, and chronic conditions.

🧘

Thai Massage

Stretch, restore, and re-energise.

Combines gentle stretching, acupressure, and rhythmic compression to improve flexibility, relieve tension, and boost energy levels. Performed fully clothed on a comfortable mat.

🌸

Swedish / Relaxation Massage

Unwind completely with flowing, soothing strokes.

Designed for total relaxation. Long, flowing strokes calm the nervous system, ease muscle tension, and leave you feeling refreshed and restored.

🏃

Sports Massage

Perform better. Recover faster.

Targeted treatment for active bodies. Helps prevent injury, speed recovery, and improve performance — whether you're a weekend runner or a competitive athlete.

🦶

Foot Reflexology

Restore balance from the ground up.

Applies targeted pressure to specific points on the feet, stimulating the body's natural healing response. A deeply relaxing treatment that benefits your whole body.

💧

Lymphatic Drainage Massage

Gentle detox and immune support.

Light, rhythmic strokes encourage lymphatic flow, reduce fluid retention, and support your immune system. Ideal after surgery, during illness recovery, or for general wellness.

🧠

Head & Neck Massage

Instant relief for tension headaches and tight shoulders.

Focused treatment for the head, neck, and upper shoulders — perfect for desk workers, screen fatigue, and stress-related tension. Quick, effective, and deeply satisfying.
