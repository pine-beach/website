About Merrylands Physiotherapy

Merrylands Physiotherapy has been caring for the Western Sydney community since 1996. Our approach goes beyond symptom relief — we investigate and treat the root factors behind your condition, including posture, lifestyle, work demands, and movement patterns. Our highly qualified therapists offer a wide range of services under one roof: from general physiotherapy and sports injury management to remedial massage, dry needling, clinical pilates, and more. We give every patient the knowledge and tools to manage their health long-term.
