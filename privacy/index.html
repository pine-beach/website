We've written this in plain language because we think privacy policies that require a law degree to understand aren't actually serving anyone. If something's unclear, email us.

Pine Beach Pty Ltd ("Pine Beach", "we", "us", "our") operates pinebeach.com.au and sells digital products including Business in a Box, Team in a Box, Brand in a Box, and Daily Operations. This policy explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, who we share it with, and how you can exercise your rights.

We are an Australian entity and comply with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

What we collect

We collect only what we need to run the business. Here's the complete list:

When you sign up to our newsletter:

  • Your email address — that's it.

When you make a purchase:

  • Your email address (for delivery and receipts)
  • Payment information — processed directly by Gumroad or Stripe; we never see or store your card details
  • Your name, if you provide it at checkout
  • Country, for tax compliance purposes

When you contact us:

  • Your email address and whatever you include in your message

Automatically, when you visit the site:

  • Basic analytics data (page views, referrer, general location) if analytics are active — see Cookies below
  • Standard server logs (IP address, browser type, request timestamps) — retained briefly for security purposes

We don't collect: biometric data, health information, government identifiers, or anything else that isn't directly relevant to delivering our products and support.

How we use it

We use your information for these specific purposes:

  • Order fulfilment — delivering your purchase and sending receipts
  • Product updates — notifying you when we ship updates to products you've purchased (lifetime updates, as promised)
  • Newsletter — sending product news and updates, if you subscribed
  • Customer support — responding to your questions and resolving issues
  • Security and fraud prevention — protecting the business and our customers
  • Legal obligations — tax records, compliance requirements

We don't sell your data. We don't use it for advertising. We don't profile you for targeting. The data you give us gets used for the reason you gave it.

Third parties

We use a small number of third-party services to operate. Here's who they are and what they handle:

  • Gumroad — our primary sales platform. Processes purchases, handles payment, and manages the customer relationship for orders placed via Gumroad. Gumroad's privacy policy governs their handling of your data. gumroad.com/privacy
  • Stripe — payment processing. If you checkout via Stripe, your payment details go directly to Stripe's servers and are governed by their privacy policy. We never store card numbers. stripe.com/au/privacy
  • Vercel — website hosting. Vercel hosts pinebeach.com.au and processes standard web request data (IP addresses, request logs) as part of serving the site. vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

We don't share your data with anyone else. We don't use advertising platforms, data brokers, or third-party marketing tools that would receive your personal information.

Cookies

We keep cookies minimal. Here's what's actually on pinebeach.com.au:

  • Essential cookies — used by Vercel for routing and security. These are necessary for the site to work and can't be opted out of.
  • Analytics cookies — if we use an analytics tool (currently evaluating), it will be a privacy-first option (e.g. no cross-site tracking, no fingerprinting, data stored in AU/EU). We'll update this section when that's live.

We don't use advertising cookies, remarketing pixels, or third-party tracking scripts. Your browsing habits on this site aren't being sold or shared.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Essential functionality may be affected, but nothing beyond that.

Data retention

We keep your data for as long as it's needed and no longer:

  • Newsletter subscribers — until you unsubscribe. Every email has an unsubscribe link.
  • Purchase records — seven years, as required by Australian tax law (ATO requirements for financial records).
  • Support emails — until the issue is resolved, then typically up to two years in case follow-ups are needed.
  • Server logs — 30 days, for security monitoring.

When the retention period expires, we delete or anonymise the data.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention obligations)
  • Unsubscribe — opt out of marketing emails at any time (every email has an unsubscribe link)
  • Complain — lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) if you believe we've mishandled your data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@pinebeach.com.au. We'll respond within 30 days.

Australian Privacy Act compliance

Pine Beach Pty Ltd is an Australian entity operating under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth). We comply with the thirteen Australian Privacy Principles (APPs), which govern how we collect, use, disclose, store, and give access to personal information.

If you have a complaint about how we've handled your personal information and we haven't resolved it to your satisfaction, you can contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC):

  • Website: oaic.gov.au
  • Phone: 1300 363 992
  • Post: GPO Box 5218, Sydney NSW 2001

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll update the date at the top of this page and, if significant, notify newsletter subscribers. The current version always lives at pinebeach.com.au/privacy.

Questions? Just ask.

Privacy questions, data requests, or anything that seems off — email us directly. We'll respond promptly and in plain language.
